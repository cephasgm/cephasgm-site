Water‑Energy IoT System

I build intelligent IoT systems that optimise water pumping using real‑time electricity tariff data, water levels, and leak detection. The system uses an ESP32 microcontroller with ultrasonic sensor, current/energy sensors, and a cloud backend (Flask, MySQL) to automate pumps during off‑peak hours, saving up to 30% energy and preventing water waste. Includes a reactive dashboard, manual override, email alerts, CSV reports, and PWA.

Related Projects:

  • CephasGM Water‑Energy Management - Live system at water.cephasgm.org
  • ESP32 firmware & Flask API - Open source (available on request)
  • Tariff‑aware pump controller - Up to 30% energy savings

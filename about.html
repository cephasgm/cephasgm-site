About Cephas GM

Building innovative solutions for enterprise, safety, AI, IoT, and digital transformation

Our Mission

To develop and deploy world-class enterprise solutions, AI platforms, safety systems, IoT water‑energy optimisation, and digital tools that enable businesses and individuals to operate more efficiently, safely, and sustainably.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of integrated business management solutions, multimodal AI platforms, industry-specific IoT systems, and digital transformation tools that transform how organisations work and grow.

Current Projects & Focus

  • CephasGM-ERP: Comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning system for business operations management. (Demo)
  • CephasGM-AI: Fully functional multimodal AI platform (conversational AI, image/video generation, voice search, text-to-speech, coding assistant). ai.cephasgm.org
  • CephasGM-VPN: Next‑gen WireGuard & anti‑DPI VPN service for privacy and censorship bypass. vpn.cephasgm.org
  • Health-Safety-PWA: Occupational health and safety system for the metal management industry. (Live App)
  • cephasgm-portal: Secure client and staff portal with personalised dashboards. (Portal Access)
  • smart-location-tracker: Advanced asset and personnel tracking PWA. (Tracker Demo)
  • SignAid: Universal sign language translation platform (in development). Preview
  • CephasGM Water‑Energy Management System: IoT‑based optimisation for water pumping using real‑time tariff data, leak detection, and energy monitoring. Saves up to 30% on electricity bills. water.cephasgm.org
  • cephasgm-site: Corporate digital presence at cephasgm.org.

Future Plans

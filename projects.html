CephasGM Water‑Energy Management System

Overview

An intelligent IoT system that optimises water pumping using real‑time electricity tariff data, water level, and leak detection. Built on ESP32, Flask, MySQL, and a reactive JavaScript dashboard. Saves up to 30% energy by pumping only during off‑peak hours. Supports two hardware modes: Basic (current sensors) and Modern (flow meter + whole‑house energy meter). Includes AI assistant, manual remote control, billing reports, and PWA.

Key Features:

  • Real‑time water level, flow, pressure & energy analytics
  • Tariff‑based pump automation (off‑peak optimisation)
  • AI‑based leak detection & email alerts
  • Manual override & historical trends (last 60 records)
  • CSV report export & simulation speed control
